Global GDP, FDI and BOP visualisations From 2010 to 2020 (Using Power BI) - click here to view the data used

In this project, I aimed to develop an interactive dashboard to visualise global data of Gross Domestic Product (GDP), Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) and Balance of Payments (BoP). The period of the data is from 2010 to 2020 and I acquired it from the World Bank Data Bank. Power BI visualisations for Ukraine & Russia Food Commodities - click here to view the data used

The war between Ukraine and Russia has had a negative domino effect across multiple regions of the world both economically and politically. However, this project aimed to investigate how the war has affected key food commodities that come from Ukraine and Russia. The key commodities produced and exported from Ukraine and Russia are wheat, barley, maize, and sunflower oil. The source of the data was from Our World in Data, Business Insider and Investing.com. I used Power BI to create a time series of the commodity prices and bar charts to visualise Ukraine's and Russiaâ€™s share of global production and exports and highlight the top ten countries that import the most as a ratio to the domestic supply.
