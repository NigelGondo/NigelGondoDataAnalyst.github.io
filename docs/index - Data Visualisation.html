Global GDP, FDI and BOP visualizations From 2010 to 2020 (Power BI) - click here to view the data used

In this project, I aimed to develop an interactive dashboard to visualize global data of Gross Domestic Product (GDP), Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) and Balance of Payments (BoP). The period of the data is from 2010 to 2020 and I acquired it from the World Bank Data Bank. In the project, I used Power Query to do minor data transformations and I created measures by using DAX to create KPIs. When viewing the project make use of the filters available, Year and Country, to better interact with the data.

Analysis of Ukraine & Russia Food Commodities (Power BI) - click here to view the data used

The war between Ukraine and Russia has had a negative domino effect across multiple regions of the world both economically and politically. However, this project aimed to investigate how the war has affected key food commodities that come from Ukraine and Russia. The key commodities produced and exported from Ukraine and Russia are wheat, barley, maize, and sunflower oil. The source of the data was from Our World in Data, Business Insider and Investing.com. I used Power BI to create a time series of the commodity prices and bar charts to visualise Ukraine's and Russiaâ€™s share of global production and exports and highlight the top ten countries that import the most as a ratio to the domestic supply.

ANALYSIS OF GOVERNMENT PERFORMANCE BY REGION AND COUNTRY (Power BI) - click here to view the data used

The purpose of this project was to create interactive dashboards that are there to illustrate government performance both at a regional and national level. The project period focused on the pre-pandemic (2017-2019) and post-pandemic (2020-2022) period. The metrics of focus were general government revenue, general government total expenditure and general government gross debt. Also, gross domestic product (GDP) and inflation were additional metrics included for further context. I sourced the data from IMF - World Economic Outlook Database

Analysis of trade and investment by continent and country (Power BI) - click here to view the data used

Trade and investment have been the lifeblood of the global economy for decades, and with the recent conclusion of the World Economic Forum (WEF) meeting in Davos 2023, trade and investment was one of the key agendas of this gathering. In essence, this project aimed to get an insight into the progress of trade and investment in the global economy. The project's period is from 2013 to 2021. The metrics focused on were commercial services and merchandise products exported and imported, and foreign direct investment (FDI) both inflows and outflows. I sourced the data from the World Trade Organization (WTO) and The World Bank.

Analysis of Express Entry Statistics (Power BI) - click here to view the data used

Express entry is an immigration pathway into Canada to obtain permanent residency (PR) for skilled workers in Canada and those overseas. The program started on the 1st of January 2015 and hundreds of thousands of people have been privileged to get PR since then. Therefore, the purpose of this project was to highlight the various express entry statistics, from 2015 to 2022, of the number of individuals admitted for PR based on gender, age, intended occupation, country of citizenship of the applicants and the province they intend to live in. I sourced the data from Government of Canada open government repository.

Analysis of Global tourism statistics (Power BI) - click here to view the data used

This project aimed to create reports with interactive visualizations of global tourism statistics, looking at over 200 countries and regions, that analyzed inbound, outbound, and domestic tourism statistics. Additional statistics on GDP, tourism employment, and types of tourism industries were analyzed for further context. The period of focus was from 1995 to 2021. Some of the key statistics looked at were tourism expenditure, arrivals into a country, departure to other countries, modes of transport used, purposes of visit, and so on. I obtained the data from the World Tourism Organization database.
