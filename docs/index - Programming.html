Cluster analysis is an important concept as it helps businesses understand the natural grouping of certain variables in a dataset. Especially for investors, in the case of this project, who want to understand the best portfolios when investing in the stock market. The purpose of this project was to conduct Kmeans clustering to identify the different clusters of the top thirty companies on the Johannesburg Stock Exchange (JSE).
